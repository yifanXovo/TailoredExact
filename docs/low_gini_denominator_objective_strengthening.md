# Low-Gini Denominator and Objective-Estimator Strengthening

`--compact-bc-low-gini-strengthening safe` activates already-proved low-Gini centering/domain and objective-estimator families. `--compact-bc-denominator-bound-mode tight` and `--compact-bc-objective-estimator-mode adaptive` are logged as safe-mode controls over these existing rows. `aggressive-diagnostic` is metadata-only for experiments and must not certify.
