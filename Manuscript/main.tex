\begin{abstract}
We present the stable paper-facing exact method implemented by
\texttt{paper-gf-tailored-bc}.  The method combines a Gini-frontier
decomposition, valid interval screening, and CPLEX-managed fixed-interval
compact branch-and-cut.  A relaxation vector selects bounded route-cutset
candidates, while validity of every added inequality is independent of that
vector.  A full-frontier ledger, independent incumbent verification, and
source audits determine whether a global claim is admissible.
\end{abstract}
